Smart irrigation uses sensors, weather data, and automation to deliver water to plants based on their actual needs, conserving water and energy while promoting plant health and increasing crop yields.It automates traditional irrigation systems by monitoring real-time conditions, such as soil moisture.

Drip irrigation or trickle irrigation is a type of micro-irrigation system that has the potential to save water and nutrients by allowing water to drip slowly to the roots of plants, either from above the soil surface or buried below the surface.
